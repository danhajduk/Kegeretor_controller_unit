ANSI Escape Codes for Colors:
    Black: \033[30m
    Red: \033[31m
    Green: \033[32m
    Yellow: \033[33m
    Blue: \033[34m
    Magenta: \033[35m
    Cyan: \033[36m
    White: \033[37m

Reset Formatting:
    Reset to default color: \033[0m

Bold Text:
    Bold on: \033[1m
    Bold off: \033[22m
